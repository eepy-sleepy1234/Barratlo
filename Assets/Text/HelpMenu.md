**###Gameplay###**



	**Hands:**

&nbsp;		High Card: Highest card played



&nbsp;		Pair: Two cards of the same rank



&nbsp;		Two Pair: Two Pairs



&nbsp;		Three of a Kind: Three cards of the same rank



&nbsp;		Straight: Five consecutive cards by rank



&nbsp;		Flush: Five cards of the same suit



&nbsp;		Full House: A pair with a three of a kind 



&nbsp;		Four of a Kinds: Four cards of the same rank



&nbsp;		Straight Flush: A straight of the same suit



&nbsp;		Five of a Kinds: Five cards of the same rank



&nbsp;		Flush House: A Full House of one suit 



&nbsp;		Flush Five: Five of a Kind of one suit
